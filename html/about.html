This Is Me

My life is written in movie quotes.

As a little girl, my father began traveling far across the ocean for his job. He would send home the most beautiful pictures of Geneva, Dubai, Sydney, Paris etc. The world is such a beautiful place and I knew I wanted to see it all, just like my Dad.

However, when my dad was home, he was HOME. Me and my two sisters would tackle my dad the moment he came through the door. We would sing, dance, laugh, tell stories, and eat our favorite food... Mac and Cheese.

My love for film, food, and travel, I get from my dad. I loved date nights with my dad to the theater. We would go see every movie in the theater he had missed while he was away. We talked about his travel adventures, the amazing food he ate abroad, and how each new country was a picture off of a movie screen.
